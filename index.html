How to Bid

1. Browse Artist Alley Artworks

Explore the collection of artwork currently on display in Artist Alley. Each piece includes detailed information about the artist, medium, and estimated value.

2. Place Your Bid

Click on any artwork to view details and place a bid. Bids are placed in increments of $25, $50, or $100 depending on the current bid amount.

3. Monitor the Auction

Keep track of your bids and auction end times. The Artist Alley auction runs from July 16 through August 28, 2025, with live countdown timers showing time remaining.

4. Payment & Collection

Winners will be notified via email. Payment can be made by credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer. Artwork can be collected at our gallery locations.

About Artist Alley

Artist Alley showcases the vibrant creativity of our local artistic community.

Artist Alley is a special initiative of the Kokomo Art Association, featuring works from talented local artists displayed in a dedicated gallery space. This online auction allows art enthusiasts to bid on the actual pieces currently on display, bringing the gallery experience directly to your home.

Each artwork in this auction represents the creativity and skill of our vibrant artistic community. From traditional paintings to contemporary mixed media and sculpture, Artist Alley celebrates the diverse artistic expressions found in our local area.

The Kokomo Art Association has been fostering artistic expression in Howard County since 1926, and Artist Alley continues this tradition by providing a platform for local artists to share their work with the community.
