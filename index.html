How to Bid

1. Browse Artworks

Explore our curated collection of artwork from talented local artists. Each piece includes detailed information about the artist, medium, and estimated value.

2. Place Your Bid

Click on any artwork to view details and place a bid. Bids are placed in increments of $25, $50, or $100 depending on the current bid amount.

3. Monitor the Auction

Keep track of your bids and auction end times. Each auction runs for one month, with live countdown timers showing time remaining.

4. Payment & Collection

Winners will be notified via email. Payment can be made by credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer. Artwork can be collected at our gallery locations.

About Kokomo Art Association

Our mission is to bring art to the community by bringing the community to the art.

Founded in 1926, the Kokomo Art Association has been a cornerstone of the local arts community for nearly a century. We are dedicated to fostering artistic expression, education, and appreciation throughout Howard County and beyond.

Our online auction features works from talented local artists who are members of the association. Each piece represents the creativity and skill of our vibrant artistic community.
